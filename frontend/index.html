How it works

Make Consent
Listenable

People skip legal text because it is long, dense, and not in their language. Consent Companion turns the document into a conversation before they sign.

40s
Upload, scan risk, and ask the first question before regret becomes a six-month problem.
5+
Indian languages supported for voice-first legal access without typing or legal vocabulary.
01 - Ingest
📄

Upload the fine print

Drop a PDF or paste clauses. The app chunks the document, classifies clause types, extracts money/time values, and builds a searchable risk map.

02 - Ask
🎙

Ask like a person

Say “Can they charge me every month?” or ask in Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, or Telugu. The system finds the matching clause even when your words do not match the legal text.

03 - Hear
💡

Listen before signing

Get the answer spoken back with red flags, yearly cost conversions, contradictions, and a concrete “before you sign” checklist.
